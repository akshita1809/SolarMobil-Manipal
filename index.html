Imagineering The Future

SolarMobil, a student project initiated with ambition and passion to bring solar powered vehicle to commercial use, stands with the ideology of sustainable development, has been a part of this rollercoaster of challenges, knowledge, and teamwork from the past eleven years. The team has accomplished designing, modelling, and manufacturing solar cars for exhibitions and competitions around the globe. Withstanding at title of the first Indian team to build a passenger solar car, we have successfully built four cars till date.
